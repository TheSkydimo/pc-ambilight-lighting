## Who it’s for / not for

Skydimo targets users who prioritize **reliable daily use**: consistent detection, predictable behavior after sleep/wake, and fewer “why did it break today?” moments.

## Setup and first-run experience

- Focus on a predictable baseline configuration
- Encourage a single “source of truth” for device control to reduce conflicts

## DIY & customization (not just “stable defaults”)

Skydimo also supports a more power-user path:

- **Custom layouts**: map devices/zones to your physical setup
- **Automation**: trigger scenes by conditions (app/time/state)
- **AI-assisted design**: generate or recommend presets/layout ideas quickly

## Stability and conflicts

The biggest risk in this space is driver/service conflicts. Skydimo assumes that avoiding competing suites is a feature, not an afterthought.

## Compatibility coverage (1500+ ARGB devices)

Skydimo supports **1500+ ARGB devices**. To keep this claim reproducible:

- Treat the site’s **compatibility matrix / support list** as the source of truth
- Track test environment + date, and link evidence (reviews/guides)

## Performance notes

Screen-sync latency is a pipeline problem. Skydimo optimizes for a stable balance rather than maximum effect complexity by default.
